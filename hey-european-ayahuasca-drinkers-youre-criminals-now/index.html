Hey European Ayahuasca Drinkers, You're Criminals Now!

You may have missed it while tasting nirvana from a criminalized cup, but on May 6 2014 the European Court on Human Rights on May said that religious freedom rights do not protect ayahuasca. Ms. Maria Fränklin-Beentjes was 44 years old when authorities siezed 120 liters of ayahuasca from her house in the little dutch town of Zutpen on March 2 1999.
